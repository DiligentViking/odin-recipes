Ground Beef and Rice with Cheese

Description

This is a highly palatable dish of cheese on top of a beef, rice, and tomato soup mixture [flobw]

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 8 slices processed cheese food (can you call that food?)

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 400 degrees. Grearse a 2-quart baking dish
  2. Combine cooked ground beef and tomato soup
  3. Spread 1/3 of the rice into the bottom of the prepared baking dish and cover with 1/3 of the ground beef mixture. Place 2 slices of processed cheese over the meat, then cover with 2 more layers of rice and meat. Arrange the remaining 6 slices of cheese over the top. [i'd say maybe just have two layers]
  4. Bake until casserole is heated through and cheese is bubbly, about 20 mins.
